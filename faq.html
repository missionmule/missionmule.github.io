FAQ

Contact us at info@missionmule.com if you have any further questions and we'll do our best to answer them!

  • Is the UAS autonomous?

    Yes! We've worked hard to make sure that operators only need to press "go" to run a mission.

  • Does this system work with any sensor?

    We've developed our system to be compatible with most sensors in the field today. Using our weatherproof "Data Station", we plug into existing sensors in the field and enable them to wirelessly transmit to the drone overhead. Shoot us an email and we'll be happy to work with your team to ensure that our systems are compatible.

  • What is the range of your UAS?

    Our latest generation UAS can service an area up to 80km in diameter from the launch point. That said, our system is compatible with most off-the-shelf UAS's meaning that a longer range is possible if needed. Get in touch and we'll see what we can do!

  • I've never flown a UAS before, can I benefit from Mission Mule?

    Certainly. In fact, we assume that our clients have no experience flying UAS's. We offer an end-to-end solution including a drone operator to service all data stations and deliver the retrieved data on a regular basis.

  • How often can I receive data from my remote sensors?

    Depending on client needs, we can service the entire sensor network up to once per day. For most applications, however, we recommend servicing the network at least once per month.
