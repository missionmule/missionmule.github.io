Features

Fully autonomous from takeoff to landing

The UAS autonomously flies missions to collect the data. With a serviceable range of over 40km on a single flight, the UAS does the hard work so you don't have to.

Secure storage and data transfer

All data is stored, transferred, and delivered securely using military-grade encryption. You can be confident transmitting data from any sensor, no matter how sensitive.

Long-term deployment ready

Our system was originally designed for some of the harshest conditions on the planet—the remote wadis of the UAE. Each data station is solar powered and fully weatherproof, and each one is guaranteed to survive at least 12 months without human contact.

Compatible with most existing sensors

Mission Mule's technology is plug-and-play by design. Our data station module is compatible with most sensors that you already use in the field. To know if your specific sensor is compatible with our technology, just reach out. We'd be happy to help!

Case Study

Remote sensing for animal conservation

Mission Mule is no stranger to remote data collection. Our technology was developed alongside environmental conservationists and we continue to support conservation efforts around the world.

How It Works

1. Autonomous takeoff and flight

Preprogrammed with GPS coordinates of the data stations, it runs the mission autonomously. The UAS does the flying so you don't have to.

2. Circle above Data Station

After reaching a data station GPS position, the UAS circles overhead and wirelessly collects the data transmitted from the ground-based data station.

3. Continue the mission

The UAS will continue to service all preprogrammed data stations before heading home loaded with your data.
